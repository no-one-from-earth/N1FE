Contact Us

At No One from Earth, we believe in the power of sound to transcend boundaries and forge unexpected connections. If you've found your way here, it probably means you're curious, inspired or maybe already resonating with the same frequencies we are.

We welcome artists, curators, venues, organizers and curious minds who are drawn to immersive sonic experiences and experimental performance. Whether you’d like to propose a collaboration, inquire about our curated music performances, invite us to your space, or simply say hello. We’re always open to hearing from you.

Our work thrives on connection. Each message, idea, or introduction has the potential to become part of the evolving constellation that is No One from Earth. We are rooted in the Netherlands, but our ears and minds are tuned to the global spectrum of sound.
