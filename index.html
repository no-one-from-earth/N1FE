About

Embark on a sonic journey where the boundaries of sound dissolve and the ordinary is left behind. No One from Earth brings together visionary artists, creating unconventional soundscapes that stretch the limits of musical imagination. These artists craft immersive atmospheres, blending diverse genres and experimental techniques, to transport listeners to realms far beyond our own. Prepare for a voyage where the familiar becomes strange, and the unknown unfolds in unexpected ways.

Mission

To activate portals of sonic exploration through curated music performances that feature boundary, defying artists based in the Netherlands. No One from Earth exists to disrupt the ordinary and forge immersive, otherworldly soundscapes that invite listeners to step beyond known dimensions.

Vision

To emerge as a sonic nexus for the future, an evolving platform where experimental sound artists converge to reshape perception, challenge sonic conventions and transmit signals from the edge of musical imagination.
