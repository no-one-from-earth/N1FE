About

Embark on a sonic journey where the boundaries of sound dissolve and the ordinary is left behind.

No One from Earth brings together visionary artists through curated music performances that create unconventional soundscapes and stretch the limits of musical imagination. These artists craft immersive atmospheres, blending diverse genres and experimental techniques to transport listeners to realms far beyond our own. Prepare for a voyage where the familiar becomes strange and the unknown unfolds in unexpected ways.

Our Vision

To become a living portal for sonic exploration, a space-time rift where the boundaries of music, perception, and imagination are constantly being rewritten. No One from Earth envisions itself as an ever-evolving nexus rooted in the Netherlands, where experimental sound artists converge not only to perform, but to forge new languages of listening. We seek to dissolve the limits between the known and the unknown, the acoustic and the digital, the present and the speculative.

Through curated performances that defy convention, we aim to cultivate a listening culture that is immersive, intentional, and transformative, one that invites audiences to momentarily step outside of linear time and into resonant, otherworldly soundscapes. These sonic rituals are not merely performances, but acts of transmission, signals sent from the outer edges of musical possibility, asking: What happens when we listen beyond what we know?

In this vision, No One from Earth becomes not just a platform, but a beacon, amplifying the voices of those who challenge the mainstream, experiment without compromise and imagine the future of sound as a radical, shared experience.
